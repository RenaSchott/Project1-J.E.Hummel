Johann Erdmann Hummel

The German painter Johann Erdmann Hummel was born 11.09.1769 in Kassel and died on the 26.10.1852 in Berlin. He was a painter and teacher. His nickname was "Perspektiven-Hummel" thanks to his real good perspective painting.
